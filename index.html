QuickClear: Automated Fast-Auction Marketplace

QuickClear is a mobile-first auction platform for second-hand goods. We use AI-driven verification to ensure a spam-free, secure environment.

  • AI Vision Verification: Every listing is scanned to prevent stock-photo fraud.
  • Automated Shipping: Integrated with Easyship/DPD for real-time, accurate logistics.
  • Secure Escrow: Payments are processed via Stripe Connect and held until delivery is confirmed.
  • AI Dispute Resolution: Our proprietary AI Arbiter settles condition disputes instantly using multimodal analysis.
